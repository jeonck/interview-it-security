# Question

Why do you want to work for Samsung Austin/Taylor Semiconductor as a Security Manager?

# Answer

"Samsung is at the forefront of global innovation. Securing the infrastructure that produces the world's most advanced chips is a challenge of immense scale and responsibility. I am drawn to Samsung's commitment to excellence and its rapid growth in Texas. I want to apply my experience in high-availability server security to ensure that Samsung's intellectual property and production lines remain impenetrable."
